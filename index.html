How can I prepare for our first strategy call?

To make the session as useful as possible, it helps to come prepared with a few key insights about your business.

Helpful things to think about beforehand

  • Your product or service and the problem it solves
  • Your current target customers or market
  • How you currently acquire customers (if at all)
  • The main challenges you're facing with growth or operations
  • Tools or systems you already use (CRM, automation, marketing tools, etc.)
  • What success would look like for you in the next 6–12 months

During the call we'll typically discuss

  • Your current business model and positioning
  • Your ideal customer profile and buyer personas
  • Possible growth channels and acquisition strategies
  • Operational bottlenecks or inefficiencies
  • Opportunities to introduce automation or smarter systems

The goal of the call is to identify the highest-leverage opportunities for your business and outline the next steps.
